Stroll in Bohol

Bohol is one of the most beautiful places in South East Asia when it comes to scenic beauty. It has lots to offer you throughout your stay. The famous Chocolate Hills is the greatest pride of Bohol. Basically it's a strange group of oddly shaped hills, a lot of hills. There are 1,776 hills spread over the area. It's not easy to describe the place; it's like a piece of art. Indeed an unbelievable scenery.
